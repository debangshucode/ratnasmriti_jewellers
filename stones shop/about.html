About Us


Welcome to RATNASMRITI GEMS & JEWELLERS

Established in 1998, RATNASMRITI GEMS & JEWELLERS has proudly served the gemstone and jewelry community for over two decades. Our showroom, located in the heart of Bandel, Hooghly, is a beacon of trust and quality in the industry, dedicated to offering the finest gemstones, natural Rudraksha beads, and certified diamonds. Our legacy is built on a foundation of expertise, authenticity, and a deep commitment to customer satisfaction.
Our Collections
Gemstones: We specialize in a wide range of meticulously selected gemstones, each certified for authenticity and quality. Our extensive collection includes: - Ruby (Chuni)
- Emerald (Panna)
- Yellow Sapphire (Pokhraj)
- Blue Sapphire (Neelam)
- Hessonite Garnet (Gomed)
- Red Coral (Lal Pola/Moonga)
- Pearl (Moti/Mukto)
- Diamond
- Moonstone
- Cat's Eye
- Zircon
- Natural Rudraksha Beads

Whether you are seeking a gemstone for astrological purposes, jewelry making, or personal collection, RATNASMRITI GEMS & JEWELLERS has something to meet every need and preference.
Quality Assurance
At RATNASMRITI GEMS & JEWELLERS, quality is our hallmark. We prioritize customer satisfaction and ensure that every gemstone in our collection undergoes rigorous testing processes. Our gemstones are certified by both government and international laboratories to guarantee authenticity and value.
